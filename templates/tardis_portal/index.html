Store and describe your data with Squirrel

Squirrel allows researchers to...

  • Store large amounts of data on fully backed-up, institutional storage
  • Organise data into experiments and datasets
  • Describe data, including discipline-specific aspects in detail for search and display
  • Share data online with individuals groups
  • Publish data to the world including a receiving DOIs for citation
  • Register your data with the Australian National Data Service's public index Research Data Australia


No need to sign up. Just log in with your regular Monash Authcate username.

For more info, please see the User Guide.
